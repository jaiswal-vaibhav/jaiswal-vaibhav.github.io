Dr. Vaibhav Jaiswal

Senior Researcher in Reactor Physics and Neutronics at ASNR, France

About

Dr. Vaibhav Jaiswal is a senior researcher at the neutronics and criticality safety department of the French Authority for Nuclear Safety and Radiation Protection (ASNR). He has more than ten years of experience in nuclear data, covering differential experiments, nuclear data evaluation, data processing, and benchmarking for reactor physics and criticality safety studies.

His research work began at ASNR (then IRSN), where he carried out a PhD on the development of thermal scattering cross-section data. In this work, he performed double-differential time-of-flight measurements and conducted atomistic simulations to produce high-fidelity nuclear data for neutron transport applications. After completing his PhD, he continued his research as a post-doctoral fellow at the French Alternative Energies and Atomic Energy Commission (CEA), and later returned to ASNR as a researcher.

He holds a bachelor's degree in Physics and an M. Tech in Nuclear Science and Technology from the University of Delhi, India, as well as a master's degree in Nuclear Engineering from the French National Institute for Nuclear Science and Technology (INSTN) and CentraleSupélec, one of France's leading engineering schools.

Dr. Jaiswal's expertise lies in nuclear data development, qualification, and the integration of evaluated data into neutron transport simulations. He also works on uncertainty quantification, sensitivity analysis, and the development and processing of covariance matrices to support the propagation of uncertainties in neutronic calculations. He collaborates with research institutes and international partners, contributing to the JEFF project and several expert groups. He has contributed to national and international R&D initiatives, and supervises PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and interns working in reactor physics and neutronics.

Research Topics

⚛️

Nuclear Cross Section Data

Investigating neutron interactions within nuclear reactors, focusing on measurement, processing, and evaluation of cross section data essential for reactor design and safety analysis.

🌡️

Thermal Scattering Law

Developing thermal scattering laws for various materials to accurately simulate neutron behavior at thermal energies, improving the fidelity of neutron transport calculations.

📊

Uncertainty Analysis

Assessing the impact of uncertainties in nuclear data on reactor physics calculations, contributing to the optimization of nuclear safety and performance.

🔬

Neutronics

Advancing neutronics through detailed simulations and experiments to enhance the understanding of neutron behavior and reactor kinetics.

🛡️

Criticality Safety Research

Focusing on the prevention of accidental criticality in nuclear facilities by analyzing and ensuring safe handling, storage, and disposal of fissile materials.
