About Me

Dr. Vaibhav Jaiswal is a research engineer at the French Institute for Radiological Protection and Nuclear Safety (IRSN), France. In this capacity, he leverages his expertise in the development of nuclear cross section data and Monte Carlo codes, with a specific focus on thermal scattering cross section data for moderator materials. His expansive knowledge of nuclear data is instrumental in his work on Monte Carlo code development at IRSN, marking a significant contribution to the institute's research capabilities.
